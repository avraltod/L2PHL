Money from elsewhere is a Christmas story, and a monthly one

The panel began measuring transfers consistently at Round 3, in January, the month households receive holiday money from relatives at home and abroad. OLD of households reported receiving money through a transfer service that month. The share then settled near a quarter, at OLD by Round 8. The Christmas inflow is a spike on a standing base, not a one-time event.
