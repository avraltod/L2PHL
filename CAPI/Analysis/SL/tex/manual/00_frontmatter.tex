\section*{Motivation}

L2Phl (Listening to the Philippines) is a nationally representative household
survey reaching about 108.7 million Filipinos through 2,470 households and
10,496 members across 18 regions. It runs in two modes. A CAPI baseline of 14
modules is administered face-to-face over September and October 2025 and is now
frozen; this baseline also serves as the sampling frame. A CATI panel of 10
modules is administered by monthly phone rounds R1 through R9 from November 2025
to July 2026 and rolls forward as new rounds close. The purpose of this manual
is to let a new team operate, maintain, and replicate every stage of the
workflow. The internal project codename is Project TIPON.

\begin{notebox}{Scope of this document}
This is the operational manual --- it documents how the survey is designed,
processed, weighted, and quality-controlled so that a new team can replicate and
maintain it. It is not a results report; substantive findings live in the
storylines, dashboards, and deep dives cataloged in the Deliverables sections.
\end{notebox}
